LEGAL CONCLUSIONS

All notice and jurisdictional requirements set forth in Education Code sections

44949 and 44955 were met.

Cause exists to reduce the number of certificated employees at the Los Altos

School District due to the reduction or discontinuance of particular kinds of services pursuant
to Education Code section 44955. The cause relates solely to the welfare of the schools and
the pupils thereof within the meaning of Education Code section 44949.
